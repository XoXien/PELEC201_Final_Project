FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS
What type of events do you manage?
We handle a wide range of events including Weddings, Debut, Birthday, Anniversaries and Reunion, Children, Thetned parties, Company Events, Pageants and Concerts, Advertising and Marketing Events, Product Launching, Promotion, Exhibit, Conferences, and Training Workshops.
What services do you offer?
We offer ON-THE-DAY COORDINATION, BUDGETARIAN PACKAGE, and THREE-IN-ONE SUPERSAVER DEALS including program detailing, supplier coordination, bridal entourage flowers, emcee services, and various freebies.
Do you require a downpayment and is it refundable?
Yes, a non-refundable reservation fee (30–50%) is required to secure your booking, with the remaining balance due before the event date.

You can contact us via our official Facebook page, phone number, or email for faster response and booking assistance.
